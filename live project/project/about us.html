About Tata Motor

Tata Motors Limited, a USD35 billion organisation, is a leading automobile manufacturer with a portfolio that includes
a wide range of cars, utility vehicles, trucks, buses and defence vehicles. Our marque can be found on and off-road in over
125 countries around the globe.
